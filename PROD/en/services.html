Services

This page is to describe the services provided by NDI, outlining the different steps along the value chain (Sales & Marketing, Implementation & Integration, Monitoring & Operations). Across all steps, each client is assigned a dedicated account delivery manager.

Assessment and Design

  • Build demos, showcases
  • Build MVP
  • Develop Business Case
  • Develop Implementation Plan
  • Perform Organizational alignment

Implementation & Integration

  • Perform Implementation
    • Compile list of events triggering handover to human agen
    • Define and implement integration with user data of logged in customer, agree on desired level of hyper personalization
    • “Hypercare phase”: review of chatbot-client interactions by a live agent at all times
  • Adapt content
  • Perform Training and Enablement
    • Define chatbot training data (website content, additional product documentation, golden Q&A source, …) and chatbot hyperparameters (temperature, …)

Monitoring & Operations

  • Provide Monitoring & Reporting
    • Realtime monitoring of chat and voice channels
    • Non-Realtime monitoring of email channel
  • Manage and maintain Content
    • Improve website content (and other supplementary content sources if applicable), by addressing and closing detected content gaps and inconsistencies
    • Maintain and continuously update “best possible answer” database
  • Train and improve Model
    • Collect and evaluate customer feedback
    • Run sentiment analysis of client-chatbot interactions
    • Based on customer feedback and sentiment analysis, and in agreement with the client, adjust chatbot settings, human agent handoff events, chatbot hyperparameters, etc.
  • Maintain Technology
    • Patches (Security, other)
    • Minor Releases
    • Major Releases (not included in per transaction price)
