The Intelligence Architects

We help companies embrace the power of Generative AI and be a partner for the continuous operations and evolution of Generative AI solutions, providing high-quality knowledge and asset-based services.

With the huge advancements in Generative AI technology around ChatGPT, AI Assistants have become much more powerful and evolved into a highly relevant tool for client interaction. NDI is a service provider for mid-sized companies that implements and operates customer-facing AI Assistants using the available contents of the company website and other data sources turning them into a powerful effective client conversation. NDI is permanently monitoring and evolving the AI Assistant to ensure full client satisfaction. NDI uses the power of shared services to bring working modules and solutions sharing the competency across a broader client base. NDI permanently creates input to improve the client's customer-facing contents. NDI is offering a rapid ready-to-use prototype of the AI Assistants to fast track implementations. The pricing model of NDI is based on the actual usage and therefore strictly linked to the client business case.
