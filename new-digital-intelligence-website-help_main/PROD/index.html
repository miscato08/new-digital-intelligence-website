This is us

With the huge advancements in Generative AI technology around ChatGPT, Chatbots become much more powerful and evolved into a highly relevant tool for client interaction. Midsized clients typically lack the competency and capacity to implement and permanently monitor and evolve customer-facing ChatBots. NDI is a service provider for mid-sized companies that implements and operates customer-facing ChatBots using the available contents of the company website and other data sources turning them into a powerful effective client conversation. NDI is permanently monitoring and evolving the ChatBot to ensure full client satisfaction. NDI uses the power of shared services to bring working modules and solutions sharing the competency across a broader client base. NDI permanently creates input to improve the customer’s (website) content. NDI uses the established platforms Enterprisebot. To acquire new clients, NDI is developing free of charge a ready-to-use prototype of the Chatbot for the client and presents it to them for their review and implementation decision. The pricing model of NDI is based on the actual customer interaction based on value of the interaction (e. G. sales) or cost savings (employee hours saved).

The Generative AI ChatBot Service provides customers with a GenAI-based Chatbot for their websites to answer client inquiries. The service includes the initial setup and the ongoing monitoring and operations. Also the evolution and adoption of the solution to the (frequent) technology and service changes in the market. The focus is to permanently improve the client interaction provided by the bot. For this purpose, content gaps and quality improvements in the underlying content are permanently spotted and addressed together with the clients. The bot will be integrated with human-agent interaction and platforms. Customer questions and customer feedback as well as sentiment are permanently collected, measured and evaluated to improve the quality of the customer interaction. The service is available for anonymous as well as logged-in users.

The service will be based on the leading technology platforms and will utilise their data privacy and security concepts along with their legal terms. In addition for the implementation and managed services industry standard terms and security methods will apply. The capabilities of the chat platform will be used to ensure that content from users is not shared with other users through the model/service. NDI will help the clients to use setup and operate the ChatBot in a compliant way, fulfilling the specific requirements of the European and national data privacy regulations.
